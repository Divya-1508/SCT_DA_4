#  Marketing Campaign Analysis using Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

##  Project Overview

This project focuses on performing **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** on a digital marketing campaign dataset to evaluate campaign performance and provide business-driven recommendations. Rather than building a Machine Learning model, the objective is to understand marketing effectiveness by analyzing campaign metrics, visualizing the customer funnel, calculating Return on Investment (ROI), and identifying which marketing channels deserve higher budget allocation.

---

##  Objective

- Clean and inspect the dataset
- Perform Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Visualize marketing campaign performance
- Analyze the marketing funnel
- Calculate Return on Investment (ROI)
- Identify high-performing marketing channels
- Provide data-driven budget allocation recommendations

##  Key Insights

- Google Search generated the highest total revenue among all marketing platforms.
- Google Search achieved the highest Return on Investment (ROI), making it the most profitable channel.
- Meta and TikTok generated moderate revenue but delivered lower ROI compared to Google Search.
- LinkedIn and Google Display recorded the lowest ROI despite advertising expenditure.
- The marketing funnel highlighted a significant drop from impressions to conversions, indicating opportunities for improving campaign effectiveness.
- Revenue trends varied across months, suggesting seasonal fluctuations in marketing performance.

---

##  Budget Recommendation

Based on the ROI analysis:

-  Increase investment in **Google Search** campaigns due to consistently high revenue and ROI.
-  Continue investing in **Meta** and **TikTok**, while optimizing audience targeting and campaign strategies.
-  Reduce or reassess spending on **LinkedIn** and **Google Display** because of their comparatively lower ROI.
-  Adopt an ROI-driven budget allocation strategy to maximize marketing efficiency and business profitability.
